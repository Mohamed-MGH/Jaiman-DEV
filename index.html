Sanabel's 2020 Conference

Client Centricity; The Road to Greater Financial Inclusion

Dear participants,

Given the emergency situation of the outbreak of Novel Coronavirus (2019-nCoV) and the declaration of the Novel Coronavirus as a Public Health Emergency of International Concern (PHEIC) by the Director-General of the World Health Organization, the Board of Sanabel after consultation with various stakeholders has taken the decision to postpone Sanabel 2020 annual Conference which was planned to take place in Hurgada, Egypt on 10th& 11th of March 2020.

It was such a hard decision after all the preparations and reservations were made, but as the safety of all participants is our first priority, and going back home safely, with no obstacles or prospected cancellations by the aviation companies, the decision was made to postpone.

Participants are invited to cancel their flight reservations, and hotel rooms reservations, and we will be negotiating some refunds with the hotel for the rooms. More about logistics and refunds to you will be communicated very soon to you.

We look forward to your participation, once the revised dates of the Conference are rescheduled .
